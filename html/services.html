Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to choose the right RCA service, stay compliant, and protect your property from underinsurance.

Which RCA service is right for my property?

Desktop Assessments suit standard properties needing fast, cost-effective valuations. On-Site Surveys are best for complex or high-value buildings. Our 3-Year Protection plan is ideal for long-term compliance with automatic updates.

What is a desktop reinstatement cost assessment?

A desktop RCA uses remote data, property records, and construction cost modelling to deliver a fast and efficient rebuild valuation — without the need for a site visit.

When do I need an on-site survey instead of desktop?

On-site surveys are recommended for listed buildings, complex structures, non-standard construction, or high-value assets where accuracy and detail are critical.

What is included in the 3-year protection plan?

Our 3-Year Protection plan includes a full RCA plus automated annual updates, ensuring your rebuild value stays accurate and compliant as construction costs change.

Do I really need annual updates to my RCA?

Yes. Construction costs change rapidly. Without updates, your policy can quickly become inaccurate. Our automated updates keep your valuation aligned with current market rates.

Is a desktop RCA accurate enough for insurance?

For standard properties, yes. Desktop assessments provide reliable valuations using advanced modelling. However, complex properties benefit from on-site inspection for maximum accuracy.

How fast can I get my RCA completed?

Desktop assessments are typically completed within 5 days. On-site surveys and complex properties may take slightly longer depending on scope and access.

Why does an on-site survey cost more?

On-site surveys involve a full physical inspection, detailed measurement, and expert analysis of materials and structure — delivering the highest level of accuracy for complex buildings.

What happens if I choose the wrong RCA service?

We review every instruction before proceeding. If your property requires a higher level of assessment, we will advise you — ensuring your valuation is accurate and compliant.

Is the 3-year plan better value than single assessments?

Yes. The 3-Year Protection plan spreads cost while ensuring ongoing compliance and accuracy — removing the risk of outdated valuations between assessments.
